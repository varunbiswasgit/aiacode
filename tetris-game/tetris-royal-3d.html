🎲 How to Play

◆ Controls

  • ← → — Move piece left / right
  • ↑ — Rotate piece
  • ↓ — Soft drop (faster fall)
  • Space — Hard drop (instant lock)
  • P — Pause / Resume
  • Swipe — Touch / mobile gestures

◆ Scoring

  • 1 line — 100 × level
  • 2 lines — 300 × level
  • 3 lines — 500 × level
  • 4 lines (Tetris!) — 800 × level
  • Hard drop — +2 per row

◆ Dice Mode

Toggle Dice Mode before starting. Each turn you roll a die — the result is the number of left/right moves you can make that turn. Roll again each piece. Only available before a game starts.

◆ Member Word

You must enter a valid member secret word to unlock the game. The word list is managed in member-words.json and is case-insensitive.

◆ Levelling

Every 10 lines cleared raises the level by 1. Speed caps at level 10.
