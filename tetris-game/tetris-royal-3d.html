◆ How to Play ◆

◆ Objective

Stack falling Tetrominoes and clear horizontal lines to score points. The game ends when the stack reaches the top.

◆ Controls

  • ← / → — Move piece left or right
  • ↑ — Rotate piece (with wall-kick)
  • ↓ — Soft drop (faster fall, +1 pt)
  • Space — Hard drop (instant, +2 pt per row)
  • P — Pause / Resume

◆ Touch / Mobile

  • Swipe left / right — Move piece
  • Swipe down — Soft drop
  • Swipe up — Hard drop
  • Tap — Rotate piece

◆ Scoring

  • 1 line — 100 × level
  • 2 lines — 300 × level
  • 3 lines — 500 × level
  • 4 lines (Tetris!) — 800 × level

◆ Levelling

Every 10 lines cleared raises the level by 1, increasing fall speed. Speed caps at level 10.

◆ Persona

Enter your nickname, email, and a secret word. The same combination on the same browser always loads your saved scores. Clearing browser storage resets everything — no account required.
