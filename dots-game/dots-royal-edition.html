Royal Rules

  • Tap the gap between any two adjacent dots to draw a line there.
  • If your line completes the fourth side of a rectangle, you claim that box — but the turn still passes normally.
  • In classic mode, completing a box earns you another turn immediately.
  • When all lines are drawn, the player with more boxes wins.
  • ⚄ Dice Mode: Toggle Dice ON before the first line is drawn. Press 🎲 Roll at the start of your turn to get a line budget (1–6). Draw up to that many lines — boxes score but do not preserve your turn. Turn passes when the budget hits zero. Mode locks once the game starts; use New Game to change it.
