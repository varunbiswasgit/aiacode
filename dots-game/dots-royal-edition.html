Royal Rules

  • Tap one dot, then tap a neighbouring dot to draw one line.
  • Only horizontal or vertical adjacent dots can be connected.
  • If your line completes the fourth side of a square, you claim that box.
  • When you claim a box, you immediately take another turn.
  • The player with more owned boxes wins.
